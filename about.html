About MISUMI

Our Story

Founded in 1963, MISUMI has grown from a small trading company to a global leader in industrial automation components. With over 60 years of experience, we've built our reputation on quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Today, MISUMI serves customers in over 80 countries, providing high-quality components and solutions for various industries including automotive, electronics, and manufacturing.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, high-quality automation components that help businesses improve efficiency and reduce costs. We strive to be the most trusted partner in industrial automation solutions.
